Our Mission

At TerraVision, we are on a mission to revolutionize farming with smart technology. We believe in the power of smart farming to transform Nepal's agriculture.

Despite being an agricultural country with vast farmlands and hardworking farmers, Nepal still imports vegetables and crops from neighboring countries. This is not because we lack resources but because many farmers do not have access to modern farming techniques and scientific insights about their soil and climate.
